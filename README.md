Welcome to my (very rough) binding of issac quake 4 mod

Difficulty options have been replaced with the following options:

1) Issac - spawns with a basic pistol and normal health/speed
2) Maggie - spawns with extra health + armor at the cost of reduced movement speed
3) Judas - spawns with an infinite ammo rocket launcher and increased speed but massively reduced health
4) Greed - spawns enemies in waves (though good luck beating them)

Enemies now drop items that can be walked over to give the player various permanent buffs
The name of the item will show up on screen when its picked up

Shop menu - hold b to open the shop menu
Enemies give the player coins that can be used in the shop

Weapons have been reskinned

Blaster - modified to resemble base tears (autofire tears)
Machine gun - modified to resemble soy milk (rapid fire tears)
Nailgun - modified to resemble crickets body (tears throw out other tears on expiration)
Shotgun - modified to monstros lung (throws a burst of tears)
Rocket launcher - modified to sad bombs (explosions throw out basic projectiles)
Railgun - modified to dead eye (increased damage for each consecutive hit)
Hyperblaster - modified to rubber cement (tears bounce)
Grenade launcher - modified to ipcac (poison enemies)
Lightning gun - modified to Jacobs ladder (chain attack)
Dark matter gun - modified to Polyphemus (high damage piercing, loses damage when piercing)
